MissPlitty Privacy Policy

1. Sharing data

We never share your data with anyone.

2. Storing data

MissPlitty does not store your data on its servers.

3. Contacts

MissPlitty reads your contacts library for the purpose of sending expenses and calculations recaps to their emails or phone numbers. Every action is decided by the end user, MissPlitty does not automatically send emails os texts to your contacts.
